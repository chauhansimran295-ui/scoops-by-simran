Signature Flavors:

Indulge in our signature flavors, each crafted to bring joy to your taste buds: Chocolaty Delight,
Butterscotch Bliss, and Strawberry Sensation.

  • Chocolaty Delight: Made for true chocolate lovers, this flavor features ethically sourced cocoa blended into creamy perfection.
    Pair it with a warm brownie for an extra indulgence.
  • Butterscotch Bliss: Combines caramelized butterscotch pieces with velvety cream,
    offering the perfect balance of crunch and sweetness—a timeless favorite.
  • Strawberry Sensation:Bursting with fruity freshness, this flavor uses hand-picked strawberries for a naturally sweet and refreshing treat.
